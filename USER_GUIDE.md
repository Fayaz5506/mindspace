# MindSpace User Guide

Welcome to MindSpace! This guide will help you navigate and use the features of your new digital mental health journal.

## 1. Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Open the application.
2. Click on the **"Get Started"** button or navigate to **Login**.
3. Select **"Register"**.
4. Enter your **Name**, **Email**, and a secure **Password**.
5. Click **Register**. You will be automatically logged in and redirected to the Dashboard.

### Logging In
1. Navigate to the **Login** page.
2. Enter your registered email and password.
3. Click **Login**.

## 2. Dashboard Overview
The Dashboard is your central hub. Here you can see:
- A personalized greeting.
- Quick stats on your journaling streaks.
- A summary of your recent mood.
- Quick links to start a journal, log a mood, or meditate.

## 3. Key Features

### üìù Journaling
**How to write an entry:**
1. Click on **"Journal"** in the navigation bar.
2. Click the **"+"** or **"New Entry"** button.
3. Give your entry a **Title**.
4. Write your thoughts in the text area.
5. (Optional) Tag your mood for this entry.
6. Click **Save**.

### üé≠ Mood Tracking
**How to log your mood:**
1. Navigate to **"Insights"** or look for the Mood section on the Dashboard.
2. Select how you are feeling (e.g., Happy, Neutral, Sad) or use the slider.
3. Add a quick note if you want to remember why.
4. Submit. You can view your mood trends over time in the **Insights** tab.

### üßò Mindfulness & Breathing
**How to start a session:**
1. Go to the **"Mindfulness"** page.
2. Select a breathing exercise or a meditation timer.
3. Set your desired duration (e.g., 5 minutes, 10 minutes).
4. Click **Start**.
5. Follow the visual guides. The session will automatically save to your history when completed.

### üéØ Goal Setting
**How to manage goals:**
1. Go to the **"Goals"** page.
2. Click **"Add Goal"**.
3. Describe your goal (e.g., "Drink 8 glasses of water").
4. Set a target date.
5. As you progress, you can mark goals as **Completed**.

## 4. Community
Connect with others in the **"Community"** tab. You can read anonymously shared positive affirmations or share your own to uplift others.

## 5. Privacy
Your journal entries are private and visible only to you when logged in. Please remember to **Logout** if you are on a shared device.
